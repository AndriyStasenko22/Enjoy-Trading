FULL SUPPORT OF DEAL
IN CHINA

1. Manufacturers search and production inspection
We will select reliable and quality suppliers for your order, carefully checking their registration documents and business reputation. Our employee will conduct preliminary negotiations with Chinese factories and visit the production facilities of the suppliers you specified to personally check the technological state of production.

2. Legal security of the transaction
We will provide a qualitative legal study of your contract. Our specialists will take into account all the legal norms of China's legislation, as well as ensure compliance with your requirements for terms of payment, terms and methods of delivery, quality characteristics of goods.

3. Production and shipment control
We will conduct a series of intermediate quality checks during the production of your order. This stage of control makes it possible to verify that the manufacturer fully understands the technical specification and other requirements.

4. Delivery and customs clearance of the order
The managers of the company will offer you the optimal delivery option, based on the characteristics of the cargo and your requirements for terms and delivery terms. We will monitor all stages of delivery and provide transparent customs clearance of cargo in the specified time.

INSPECTION OF THE
SUPPLIER IN CHINA

1. Verification of legal reliability Specialists of Enjoy Trading will check the availability and authenticity of the registration and foundation documents, requesting all the necessary data in authorized state bodies of China.

2. Finding out the business reputation of the supplier We will gather information in regional CCIs and industry associations of Chinese manufacturers on the activities of the supplier, as well as feedback on its work.

3. Checking for an Export License Our experts will check the presence of the export license and the reliability of the reported output and export figures.

4. Inspection of production capacities Our inspector and translator of the Chinese language will personally visit the supplier's factory to get acquainted with the managers, assess the level of management and the technological capacities of the manufacturer. You can get not pre-selected and prepared samples, but copies of products directly from the conveyor or from the manufacturer's warehouse. This will give you an opportunity to assess the true quality of the products and the capabilities of the Chinese supplier.

CONTROL OF PRODUCTION
AND INSPECTION OF LOADING

Step-by-step production control

The authorized employees of Enjoy Trading will perform a number of stage-by-stage inspections in the manufacturing process. We will check the appearance, material, color, application of brand names and other parameters, which will allow you to be sure of the conformity of the manufactured products to the control samples.

The final inspection will be a control inspection of the manufactured lot. The inspectors of Enjoy Trading will conduct a check of compliance with the specified characteristics, the terms of the contract and the control sample.

Full control of shipment

Enjoy Trading ensures the presence of a qualified inspector during the shipment of the order. Our employees will carry out quantitative control, during which a certain number of boxes will be selectively opened and all copies of products in them will be inspected. Inspectors will necessarily check the equipment and compliance with the specification parameters, as well as the availability and characteristics of the package.

Documentation

All stages of the inspection will be documented and photographed. Our staff will inform you without delay if any disputable issues are revealed. Timely notification will help you find solutions and ensure that you receive the ordered goods with the required quality.
